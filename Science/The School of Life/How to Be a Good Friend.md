# Summary of How to Be a Good Friend

Source: https://youtube.com/watch?v=LGinimRIl04

The video discusses the importance of having a good friend, and provides tips on how to be one. It emphasizes the importance of knowing how to show weakness, being genuinely interested in our sorrows, and helping us think more often. The video also recommends playing the "confessions game," which is a simple game designed to facilitate more honest conversations.
