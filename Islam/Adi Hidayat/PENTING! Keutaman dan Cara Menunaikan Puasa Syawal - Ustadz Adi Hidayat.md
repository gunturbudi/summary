# Summary of PENTING! Keutaman dan Cara Menunaikan Puasa Syawal - Ustadz Adi Hidayat

Source: https://youtube.com/watch?v=kRs8Z-6W15E

Ustadz Adi Hidayat emphasizes the importance of fasting in the month of Syawal after Ramadan and encourages Muslims to take advantage of the opportunity to increase their faith and devotion by fasting six days in Syawal. He emphasizes the significance of maintaining spiritual growth and performing good deeds even after Ramadan ends, which can lead to great rewards from Allah and a state of righteousness. Hidayat reminds viewers that no one is guaranteed to see the next Ramadan, so they should strive to improve every day and continue learning in Syawal.

Detail Summary: 
00:00:00
In this section, Ustadz Adi Hidayat discusses the importance of fasting in the month of Syawal, which follows the month of Ramadan, and the various sunnah ibadah that accompany it. He highlights the hadith of the Prophet Muhammad about the significance of fasting six days in the month of Syawal after completing the fasts in Ramadan. He explains that these six days of fasting can be done consecutively or separately, depending on one's schedule. He states that fasting in the month of Syawal can bring about great rewards and blessings from Allah, and encourages Muslims to take advantage of this opportunity to increase their faith and devotion.

00:05:00
In this section, Ustadz Adi Hidayat emphasizes the importance of consistently maintaining spiritual virtues even after the month of Ramadan ends. He states that those who consistently perform good deeds and increase their worship after Ramadan are rewarded greatly by Allah. Hidayat states that by piloting this commitment towards spiritual growth, one can maintain a spiritual state similar to one's in Ramadan throughout the year which can lead to two types of happiness: the happiness felt when breaking fast during Eid, and the happiness felt when one faces Allah bringing with them the reward of their fasting. Through maintaining spiritual growth, one can continue to please Allah and avoid sin, becoming a better person altogether.

00:10:00
In this section, Ustadz Adi Hidayat highlights the importance of the Syawal month after Ramadan. He stresses that Ramadan serves as a training ground for Muslims to improve their abilities to serve Allah, and this learning must continue in the Syawal month. Ustadz Adi Hidayat reminds viewers that no one is guaranteed to see the next Ramadan, so they should strive to improve every day and ultimately pass away in a state of righteousness.
